About Alibaba.com

Founded in 1999, Alibaba.com is the leading B2B e-commerce platform for simplified global sourcing.

Alibaba.com offers AI-powered B2B sourcing solutions, from product and supplier search, online order placement, payment, inspection, logistics services, to after-sales support, providing worldwide business buyers with a more simplified, efficient, professional, and secure sourcing process to grow and succeed.

Alibaba.com also digitalizes cross-border trading solutions, from online storefront building to order management, marketing promotion, payment, fulfillment, and beyond, offering global suppliers a seamless and end-to end approach to partnering with businesses worldwide.

In today’s complex and rapidly evolving business landscape, Alibaba.com remains committed to supporting the growth of global small and medium-sized businesses, empowering them to transform through digital trade, seize new opportunities, and accelerate business growth internationally.
